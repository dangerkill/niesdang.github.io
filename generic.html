Statistical Test

For initial step I want to apply statistical testing using hypothesis and odd ratio. But first I need to clear those duplicate values first. In order to do that thing, I need to check PrimaryID of patient’s demographic to avoid bias in our data. But I don’t keep the Null values and outliers for the next section because for in this dataset a lot of patients didn’t fill or answer questions about their weight, age, and a lot of important information, and also, we can’t impute those information for building the prediction in machine learning. So, for first step I will just remove duplicates and create the target variable for my dataset by using one hot encoding. I want to show you the outcome for this step but It’s lacking space for the slide so I will only give you the answer is Opana Er is the most frequently drug cause patients dead.

I Applied Chi- square test for Hypothesis testing , Odd ratio to check the likelihood and Confident interval.
1st : ODD ratio gives me 8.075 means that the likelihood of death of patients who using Opana Er higher 8 times than other drugs.
2nd :Chi-square p-value less than 0.05 and it reject the null the hypothesis and the risk of using Opana-er is exist .
3rd :The correct interpretation of this confidence interval is that we are 95% confident that the the odd ratio for death among opana user vs non-opana user is between 7.7 and 8.4
